    Preface 

    Towards high quality manuscripts with O.N.E Author for full colour publications.

    O.N.E Author proposes new layout improvements and the choice of one colour applied to different elements of the publication.

    Working with O.N.E Author brings an opportunity to enrich the structure of publications, allows the generation of smarter outputs and enables the identification of snackable elements within the content. These new elements are meant to be reusable and shared on social media channels and webbooks.

    Discover all the features of OECD webbooks in the video below.

    This document will help OECD users to:

    • Understand how to work with O.N.E Author to produce digital outputs.

    • Create corporate documents following rules for styles, charts, images, colours and structure.

    Producing content with O.N.E Author will help you to shorten the production timeline, create more value added products and boost the visibility of your content online.

    This guide will be of great help to prepare your manuscript and obtain a valid XML. It will accompany you step by step through the drafting process and the application of styles. Further reading is included throughout the document for more information.
